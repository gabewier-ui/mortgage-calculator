How the Refinance Break-Even Calculation Works

Refinancing only makes sense when your monthly savings outweigh your closing costs over time.

The break-even point tells you how many months it will take to recover the cost of refinancing.

If you plan to stay in your home longer than the break-even period, refinancing may save you money.

Even a small interest rate reduction can result in significant long-term savings over the life of the loan.
